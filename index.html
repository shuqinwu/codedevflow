Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for teams and users evaluating our local productivity app capabilities.

Our core product model does not require mandatory account creation for local data usage. We prioritize direct, private, and frictionless access.

Where is user data stored?

User data is stored in local encrypted databases on the user device. We avoid unnecessary upload and preserve user ownership by design.

We focus on North America and Europe, with product and policy adaptation aligned to international compliance requirements.
